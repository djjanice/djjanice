About Me!

I originally went to college for graphic design and journalism in 2003. Since then writing and stories have been a consistent thread throughout my life. I once wrote and self-published a book that included all my nieces and nephews in it as a Christmas present. I spent three years as the Editor of Tipping Points Magazine (2021-2024).

Becoming a life coach has been rolling around in my mind since at least 2021. It wasn’t until the end of 2024 that I actually started to pursue it in earnest. As much as I wish I had started sooner, I feel much better prepared than I did in 2021 when the idea was just a seedling in my mind.

I'm excited to learn from you and journey alongside as you reflect and discover if you're still in the right story.
